About Us

From 1949 our college has started its journey. And in this long run we had so many meritorious students who became top personals in verious sectors in our country. Those students who went abroad had done some excelent works and made us proud. We aim to being the best and our students have continued to do so. We hope the same will continue in the future and NDC will bring more meritorious students up in the light of knowledge.
Notre Dame College, undoubtedly one of the best college in Bangladesh. Our motto is not just to get good grades in board exams; on the contarary we focus on how we can make our students best of the best so that they can take our country forward.
